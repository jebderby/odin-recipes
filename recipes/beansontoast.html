Beans On Toast

Description

A traditional breakfast item eaten by people that are from the UK. It contains bread that is toasted, beans that are cooked. Consider using lots of butter on the toast.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of the best baked beans in town.
  • One slice of bread.
  • A lot of butter for the toast

Steps

  1. Place bread in toaster.
  2. Cook beans in microwave for two minutes on power 10.
  3. Spread butter generously on the toasted bread.
  4. pour beans onto toast.
  5. Eat Up!
